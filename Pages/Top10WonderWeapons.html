Top 10 Wonder Weapons in Call of Duty Zombies

Wonder Weapons in Call of Duty Zombies are unique, powerful weapons with special abilities, designed to help players survive high rounds. These include iconic weapons like the Ray Gun, Thunder Gun, and Apothicon Servant, offering high damage, crowd control, and often unpredictable, game-changing abilities. Each Wonder Weapon combines power and unique mechanics, with rankings based on their overall effectiveness, ease of use, and how well they cater to different player styles.



10
R.A.I. K-84 (Firebase Z)

The R.A.I. K-84 is a hybrid assault rifle and energy weapon that fires both rapid shots and a charged explosive blast. Its alternate firing mode slows zombies, making it highly effective for crowd control. The combination of standard rifle mechanics with a Wonder Weapon’s power makes it very player-friendly, as it offers both familiarity and a unique edge for high rounds.



9
Ray Gun (All Maps)

The Ray Gun is the most iconic Wonder Weapon, firing powerful plasma bolts that deal splash damage. It’s easy to use, deals good damage, and has a high ammo capacity. Though its splash damage can hurt the user, its straightforward functionality makes it an ideal weapon for beginners looking to survive longer rounds.
